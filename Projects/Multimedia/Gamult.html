Gamult

Gamult is an intuitive interface that makes use of a multitouch screen and a simple physics engine to bring to play several modules of the robotic (javanese) gamelan of Casa da Música.

The novel interface was premiered at Semibreve Festival 2015, in Salão Nobre of Thatro Circo.
