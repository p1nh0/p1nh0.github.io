Presence

The Primordial Absence is felt in the spine and then slides into the ground. Eden envisions itself in another dimension. Innocence here no longer exists, there is only the echo of it's idea from another time. The dilated pupil of the shape that apparently is not there, knows that silence is black. So we train ourselves the ascention with screams. "Release". Volume up. "1, 2, sound, sound ..." It takes amplification! Dreams are dragged on the floor and reverberate in the cell membrane. Interference noise, "feedback"! Tuning ... the ritual of freedom! The synthetic sound is processed with solar storms, arrhythmias and complementary colors! We scratch chest to dig up the soul and venture into a stereo path. Walls fall down, we dance with exploding popcorns and here comes the "Deus Ex Machina".
