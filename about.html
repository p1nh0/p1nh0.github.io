Tiago Ângelo
is a Portuguese artist exploring the relationships between music, technology, and sound at phenomenological and sensory levels. He often collaborates with other artists to create sound installations, musical instruments, interactive media, and musical or theatrical performances. Rooted in electronics, programming, and composition, his work invites listeners to experience sound as a phenomenon. Through his practice, Tiago seeks to deepen and expand our relationship with music and sound, fostering a dialogue between technology and the senses.
