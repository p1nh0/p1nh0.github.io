Welcome to Tiago Ângelo's homepage

I am a composer/musician, sound artist and creative coder whose work converges sound, media and interaction.

I'm currently an active member of Digitópia Collective at Casa da Música Foundation as well as Sonoscopia association. I've also been composing and developing interactive media for theatre companies such as D.E.M.O. and Radar 360o.

I also have been lecturing workshops and talks on themes such as interactive music and the development of novel musical instruments at conferences, universities and other venues such as KISMIF 2014 (Keep it Simple Make it Fast - Underground Music Scenes and DIY Cultures conference) at Faculty of Arts of the University of Porto, FIMP 2014 (Porto International Puppet Festival) and Casa da Música.

I'm also an open-source and DIY enthusiast, often building instruments and musical software for my own performances.

Born in Coimbra in 1985, I attended the Musical Conservatoire of Coimbra from 2001 to 2008 on the trombone class. Graduated in Electronic Music and Music Production at the School of Applied Arts of the Polytechnic Institute of Castelo Branco in 2010 and earned a MSc in Interactive Music and Sound Design at the Faculty of Engineering of the University of Porto in 2012.
