Tardigrada: Drums and Hipertrombone with actuated mute

Improvisation recorded for In Trux We Pux - a compilation conducted by Favela Discos , that seeks to register and expose a series of sound tendencies and collaborative practices that have been developing in the experimental music scene of Porto.
