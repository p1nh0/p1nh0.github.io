Phonospermia

Phonospermia (2023): produced by Sonoscopia. Performed by Clara Saleiro, Angélica Salvi, Henrique Fernandes, Tiago Ângelo and Gustavo Costa. Recorded live at Festival Circular, Vila do Conde, 2023. Video by Augusto Lado.

Cor Ardens

Cor Ardens (2020): Live performance at Vox Populi Gallery, Philadelphia, US. Gustavo Costa, percussion and objects. Tiago Ângelo, electronics. Video by Jim Strong.

Discography

Tardigrada

Tardigrada (2021): drums and hipertrombone with actuated mute. Improvisation recorded for In Trux We Pux - a compilation conducted by Favela Discos , that seeks to register and expose a series of sound tendencies and collaborative practices that have been developing in the experimental music scene of Porto.

Disposofónicos

Disposofónicos: Acumuladores de Objectos Sonantes (2019): sums up several years of hard work on Sonoscopia. Box set with hardcover book, 4 CD's, 1 vinyl record, 2 photos. (See credits on each track here)).
